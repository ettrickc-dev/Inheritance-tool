      ⏰ Why timing matters

      In most estate disputes, leverage is determined by who moves first — not who is technically right.

      • Someone else filing first can control everything
      • Left-out heirs can lose leverage fast
      • Wrong path = delays, extra costs, court rejections
      • Unclaimed money can sit frozen for years
